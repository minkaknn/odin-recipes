Carrot bread rolls

A recipe for Finnish carrot bread rolls. You can also make them without carrots or replace the carrots with cheese, seeds or something else you want to try.

Ingredients

  • 50 g yeast
  • 5 dl milk or water
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp sugar (or honey)
  • 50 g butter
  • 10 dl bread roll flour (all-purpose flour is okay too)
  • 3 dl grated carrots (2-3 carrots)

Steps

  1. Dissolve the yeast in lukewarm milk or water.
  2. Combine flour, salt, grated carrots, and sugar (or honey).
  3. Mix everything together and add (soft) butter. (Mixing with hands might be easier as the dough gets thicker.)
  4. Cover the dough (you can use a clean kitchen cloth) and set it aside for 45 - 60 minutes, until it has doubled in size.
  5. Roll the dough into long bars and cut these into pieces. Roll each portion into a ball and set on the baking sheets.
  6. Cover the rolls with a kitchen cloth for 20-30 minutes.
  7. Bake the rolls on the middle level of the oven for 10-15 min (225 degrees).
