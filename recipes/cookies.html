Chocolate Chip Cookies

A simple recipe for simple and delicious chocolate chip cookies.

Ingredients

  • 125 g butter
  • 1 dl sugar
  • 1 dl brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla sugar
  • 3 dl flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 150 g chocolate or chocolate chips (your choice)

Steps

  1. Whisk the room temperature butter and sugars with an electric mixer until smooth. Add the egg and vanilla sugar, whisk.
  2. Combine the flour, baking soda and salt.
  3. Add the chocolate chips or the chocolate chopped.
  4. Combine all of the ingredients.
  5. Refrigerate the dough for at least 30 minutes
