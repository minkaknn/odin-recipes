Overnight Oats

Recipe for a good breakfast: overnight oats. You can modify the recipe to your liking. The recipe is for 1 serving.

Ingredients

  • 1,5 dl milk (oat milk, almond milk, whatever you like best)
  • 1,5 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 dl oats
  • 1/3 tsp cinnamon, sugar, vanilla sugar or cacao (choose the ones that go well with your other ingredients)
  • 1/2 tbsp honey or maple syrup (choose the one you like)
  • 3 dl flour
  • berries and/or fruits of your choice

Steps

  1. Mix everything, except the fruits and berries, in a bowl or a jar.
  2. Refrigerate overnight, or until the oats and chia seeds have absorbed the milk and the porridge is thick.
  3. Mix the porridge and add berries, fruits and other toppings you like.
